Since 2005, CallPrint Limited has offered commercial high-quality printing, publishing and advertising services and has earned the loyalty of some of the top corporate houses in Ghana, giving the best quality, service and meeting committed delivery schedules. At CallPrint Limited, we believe in creating value to our customers. We understand the customer’s requirements fully, collaborate with the customer and optimize the specifications of the project – be it size, paper selection etc. So as to optimize the cost and still produce best-in-class products. CallPrint Limited is a one-stop service provider offering an entire services using state-of-the-art technology from industry leaders in the following areas.

  • Offset Printing
  • Digital Printing
  • Publishing
  • Advertising
  • T-Shirt Printing/ Embroidery
  • Branded Party Gift Packages, etc
